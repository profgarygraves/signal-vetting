  • BP 6340
    Bids and Contracts
    Vendor contracts above the Public Contract Code threshold require Board ratification before they're enforceable; sets the lowest-responsive-bidder rule and delegates contracting authority. — Read the policy ↗
  • AP 3300
    Public Records
    §5.11–5.12 exempt information-security records and vendor-identifying codes from public disclosure — relevant to how a completed assessment like this one should itself be handled. — Read the policy ↗
  • AP 3720
    Computer and Electronic Communication Systems
    §6.0 requires a written information-security program, including annual risk assessments, and requires the District to “select and retain service providers capable of maintaining appropriate safeguards” and bind them by contract to do so. — Read the policy ↗
  • AP 3722
    District Data Security Standards for End Users
    §9.0 requires any third party accessing District data to sign a Confidentiality & Nondisclosure Agreement, and requires a Data-Sharing Agreement approved by the VC of Educational Services & Technology before any data is shared. — Read the policy ↗
  • BP/AP 3310
    Records Retention and Destruction
    Sets Title 5-based retention classes (Permanent / Optional / minimum 3-year Disposable) covering District records, including electronically stored information. — Read the policy ↗
  • BP/AP 5040
    Student Records, Directory Information, and Privacy
    NOCCCD's FERPA policy — prohibits disclosure of student personally identifiable information without written consent, apart from narrow directory-information or legal exceptions. — Read the policy ↗
